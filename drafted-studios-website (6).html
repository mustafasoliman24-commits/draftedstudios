Who We Are

WE MAKE ATHLETES IMPOSSIBLE TO IGNORE

Drafted Studios is a GTA-based athlete content agency built by athletes, for athletes. We exist for the undiscovered talent — the players who have the skill but not the spotlight.

From promo shoots to hype edits to full recruitment packages, we create the content that gets you seen by scouts, coaches, and the world.

We cover every sport, every level, across the entire Greater Toronto Area.
